Our Process

Our Process

01

Discovery

The foundation of every great strategy begins with a good look under the hood. We begin by evaluating the current state of your business and market position, diving into every aspect, from your target audience, competition and comparables, to current market trends and opportunities. Don't worry, we pay close attention to what's working, as well as what isn't, to make sure we don't throw the baby out with the bathwater.

02

Idea Generation

Here's the fun part. The GoodWolf Ideas Lab is where innovation and creative projects are born. Our award-winning creatives identify the angles and stories that can effectively communicate your core value and make you stand out in a crowded market. Our approach is 360 and we love to uncover new and effective ways to extend your brand to meet consumers where they are… and in ways that trigger action.

03

Strategic Planning

Armed with our research and fresh ideas (never one size fits all) we get to work on a clear strategic plan that outlines the steps needed to achieve your business objectives and goals. This plan usually includes a detailed marketing strategy, budget, and timeline for implementation with natural GO points for you to "greenlight" new projects and initiatives.

04

Execution & Delivery

Next comes making the dream a reality. We work closely with your team to roll out the pieces, whether it's an omnichannel marketing strategy, multi-platform content program or a new product design development. All along we track results, and make adjustments to ensure that you stay track to achieve your goals. Then of course, we can stick around to help you stay ahead of the curve and continue to evolve.
